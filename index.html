Conclusion

The graphs summarizes the winning teams standing during month before the playoff, first half of the season and the full season. As seen in the graph out of all the 21 major games played in the year 2014, 14 games have winning team on the left side of the zero which indicates that those teams were not doing as well as its opponent team, thus not having winning momentum and still won the games. In the remaining 7 games, the winning teams have been doing better than their opponent team, thus having a winning momentum. The month before the final playoff have all the teams on the left side, thus winning teams did not have a winning momentum prior to the playoffs. Based on this observation, we would like to conclude that the winning momentum doesnt really matter or doesnt improve the chances of winning.
